GoPieceDrawer

val GoPieceDrawer: SgfPieceDrawer (source)

The default SgfPieceDrawer used by the SgfView to draw pieces.

It simply draws a circle of the given blackColor or whiteColor, slightly smaller than the given size, and draws a border around the piece if the whiteColor is Color.WHITE.
