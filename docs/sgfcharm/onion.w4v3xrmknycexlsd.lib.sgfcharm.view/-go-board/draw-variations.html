drawVariations

fun Canvas.drawVariations(variationData: List<VariationData>, paint: Paint): Unit (source)

Implements a simple variation markup with letters, where variations without moves are put to the middle of the board, with as equal spacing as possible, respecting other variations that might have been printed there already (i.e., those with moves).
